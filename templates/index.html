Steps to navigate through our website

Take a picture of your barcode and either upload it, or if you took an image in the app, it will be uploaded automatically(barcode must be EAN-13, as shown on the right)

Your result will now pop up on whether or not your product is recyclable

If your product is not recyclable, our website will show you existing recyclable alternatives to the product that you are buying(some products do not have recyclable alternatives)

If your product is recyclable, you will be notified of that and it will also show you some recycling centers near you that will accept your item

How it Works

You might be wondering how our site works, this is a step by step guide on how our project converts your image into recycling information!

First the product number is extracted from the image that was inputted.

The information extracted is then plugged into a database that outputs the required information about the product.

A webscraper is then used to find keywords in the information outputted to find which category the product falls into.

Based upon the category that the product falls into, the user will be informed about the recyclability of the product.
